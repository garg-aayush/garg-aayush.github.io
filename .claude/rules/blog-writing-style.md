# Blog Posts: Writing Style

## Tone
- Write in first person, conversational tone, as if explaining something to someone one-on-one
- Keep the writing natural and not overly polished. It is okay to have minor grammatical imperfections.
- Avoid AI hype language, gloating, or marketing-style adjectives. Keep reflections grounded in personal experience

## Formatting
- Do not use em dashes
- Keep titles straightforward and descriptive, not clickbait or overly dramatic (e.g. "Building Browser Tools with Claude Code" not "I Built 14 Tools Without Writing Code")
- Do not mention specific counts in titles or descriptions, they become stale as things change
- Screenshot placeholders use HTML comments: `<!-- Screenshot: description -->`
